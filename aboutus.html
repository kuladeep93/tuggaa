About us

Around the globe, in established and emerging markets, Tuggaa events generate a huge business every year. They play a vital role in building industry networks and sustaining business communities, as well as facilitating education and professional learning.

Before you make a big marketing investment, you want to know you’ll be spending it to reach the right people, and engage them the right way by delivering the right message. That’s where our Strategists come in.

We want to understand why you do events, and how you define and measure success. We start with the fundamentals: Event portfolio; scale, location, and frequency; audience segmentation and cost of acquisition; competitive landscape and positioning; and metrics strategy, data collection, and analytics. Tuggaa will help you define your audiences and figure out the best ways to engage them, and then craft experiences that resonate with your audience while supporting your brand.

What makes Tuggaa so special? It starts with our diverse and talented staff of planners, project managers, account professionals, designers, producers, strategists, technologists, finance managers and more. This team is focused on customer success and willing to work together to ensure we deliver that success every time.

Our mission


"To deliver contacts, content and communities with the power to transform your business."

Delivering contacts - the essence of what we do; bringing people together for mutual business, professional and personal benefit;

content - customers achieve more at Reed events; more information, more innovation, more education;

and communities - we draw industries and markets together under a common banner, fostering relationships;

with the power to transform - our vision is to offer each and every customer the potential for dramatic change;

your business - we are committed to helping our customers grow their business and to maximise their return on investment. This is measured through improved performance in sales, education, brand building, sourcing, solutions and networking
